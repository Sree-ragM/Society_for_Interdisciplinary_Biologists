About Us

The Society of Interdisciplinary Biologists is a dynamic community of developers, students, research scholars, faculty, and enthusiasts from the Amrita School of Biotechnology. Driven by a shared passion for the convergence of biology and technology, we are committed to fostering innovation and excellence. Our Mission Our mission is to empower the next generation of scientists and technologists by providing opportunities for interdisciplinary learning, collaboration, and research. We aim to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application, equipping our members with the skills and knowledge necessary to address global challenges.

  • 1. Mentorship : Benefit from the guidance of experienced mentors who can help you navigate your academic and professional journey. Internship and Fellowship
  • 2.Opportunities : Gain hands-on experience and explore cutting-edge research through internships and fellowships.
  • 3. Live Projects : Collaborate with peers on real-world projects and contribute to groundbreaking research.
  • 4.Workshops and Trainings : Enhance your technical skills and stay up-to-date with the latest advancements through our comprehensive training programs.
  • 5. Networking : Connect with like-minded individuals, industry experts, and potential collaborators.
